About Us

Here at the Tuscan Table Ristorante Chef Tony Lepore delivers classic Sicilian flavors with a rustic and elegant flare.

Sophisticated elevated menu in a casual environment. Using made by scratch techinques with classical culinary training Chef Tony brings to light family reciepes that have been passed down for generations.

From our table to yours. Food is Love.
